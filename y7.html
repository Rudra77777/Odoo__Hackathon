How SkillSwap Works

Get started with skill swapping in just a few simple steps

1

Create Your Profile

Sign up and create your profile. List the skills you can offer and the skills you want to learn.

2

Find & Connect

Browse or search for people with the skills you need. View their profiles and availability.

3

Send Swap Request

Send a swap request detailing what you can offer in exchange. Set up a meeting time.

4

Swap & Review

Meet and exchange skills. After your swap, leave a review to help the community.
